FAQs

When will Farlo be available?

Great question; there is no set date yet. There is still a lot of work to do and not something we want to rush.

Do you want to be part of the beta when it's ready or be notified of the official launch? Join the list!

Where will Farlo be available?

The first platforms will be iOS and MacOS. iPadOS and WatchOS will follow.

Will there be an Android or web version?

There are no plans to do so. We believe we can deliver a better and more consistent user experience by focusing our resources on building native applications.
